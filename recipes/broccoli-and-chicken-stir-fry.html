Broccoli and Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients for broccoli and chicken stir-fry

  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1 ounce water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds chicken breast cut into chuncks
  • 1 white onion, sliced
  • 3 cups of broccoli florets

How to Prepare

Follow the steps below for cooking instructions for your broccoli and chicken stir-fry.

  1. Make your sauce by stirring soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, and red pepper flakes together in a bowl. Mix water and cornstarch together in a small bowl and whisk until cornstarch is completely dissolved.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet on high heat. Add chciken and onion and cook until chicken is browned.
  3. Add broccoli to the skillet and saute until broccoli is cooked.
  4. Pour the soy sauce mixture into the bowl and stir until color is consistent.
  5. Let broccoli and chicken saute in sauce for additional 5 minutes on low heat.
